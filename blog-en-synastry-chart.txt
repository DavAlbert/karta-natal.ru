===== CONTENT (BBCode) =====
A synastry chart is the most powerful tool in relationship astrology. It compares two birth charts side by side to reveal the dynamics between two people — the magnetic attraction, emotional bonds, potential conflicts, and long-term compatibility.
[h2]What Is a Synastry Chart?[/h2]
Synastry is a branch of astrology that studies relationships by overlaying two natal charts. Unlike a simple sun sign compatibility check, a synastry chart analyzes how every planet in one person's chart interacts with every planet in the other person's chart.
The result is a detailed map of your relationship dynamics — where the sparks fly, where you feel at home, and where tensions may arise.
[h3]Synastry vs. Composite Chart[/h3]
A [b]synastry chart[/b] keeps both charts separate and looks at the connections between them. A [b]composite chart[/b] merges both charts into one, creating a single chart for the relationship itself. Both are valuable, but synastry shows how each person experiences the other.
[h2]How to Calculate Your Synastry Chart[/h2]

[list]
[*]Date of birth

The time of birth is especially important because it determines the house placements — which areas of life each person activates in the other.

[h2]Key Aspects in Synastry[/h2]
Aspects are the angles between planets in the two charts. They are the core of synastry interpretation.
[h3]Conjunctions (0°) — Fusion[/h3]
When one person's planet sits on top of the other's, the energy merges. Conjunctions are the most powerful aspects in synastry. Sun conjunct Sun creates a strong sense of identity and recognition. Venus conjunct Mars is one of the strongest indicators of physical attraction.
[h3]Trines (120°) — Harmony[/h3]
Trines create natural flow and ease. Moon trine Moon means emotional understanding comes effortlessly. Venus trine Jupiter brings joy, generosity, and shared optimism.
[h3]Squares (90°) — Tension and Growth[/h3]
Squares generate friction, but also passion. Mars square Pluto can indicate power struggles, but also intense, transformative attraction. Squares keep relationships dynamic.
[h3]Oppositions (180°) — Magnetic Pull[/h3]
Oppositions create a "you have what I lack" dynamic. Sun opposite Moon is a classic soulmate indicator — a balance between identity and emotions.
[h3]Sextiles (60°) — Opportunity[/h3]
Sextiles offer gentle, supportive energy. They indicate areas where cooperation comes easily, though the potential needs to be actively developed.
[h2]Most Important Synastry Aspects for Love[/h2]
Not all aspects carry equal weight. Here are the ones professional astrologers look at first:
[h3]Venus-Mars Aspects[/h3]
The classic attraction indicators. [b]Venus conjunct Mars[/b] is raw physical and romantic chemistry — one person embodies desire (Mars), the other embodies beauty and love (Venus). The trine and sextile versions are smoother; the square and opposition add tension but also irresistible magnetism.
[h3]Moon Aspects[/h3]
The Moon governs emotions and comfort. [b]Moon conjunct Moon[/b] means you feel emotionally at home with each other. [b]Moon conjunct Venus[/b] creates a sweet, nurturing bond. [b]Moon square Mars[/b] can mean emotional triggers but also passionate intensity.
[h3]Sun-Moon Aspects[/h3]
[b]Sun conjunct Moon[/b] is one of the strongest soulmate aspects. The Sun person illuminates the Moon person's inner world, while the Moon person nurtures and supports the Sun person's identity.
[h3]Saturn Aspects[/h3]
Saturn in synastry shows where a relationship gets serious. [b]Saturn conjunct Venus[/b] can feel restrictive but often indicates a committed, long-lasting bond. Saturn aspects determine whether a relationship has staying power or burns out quickly.
[h2]House Overlays in Synastry[/h2]
Beyond aspects, house overlays show which life areas each person activates in the other.
[h3]1st House Overlay[/h3]
When someone's planets fall in your 1st house, they have a direct impact on your identity and self-image. There is an immediate, visceral awareness of each other.
[h3]5th House Overlay[/h3]
The 5th house is romance, fun, and creativity. Planets here create a playful, joyful connection. This is the "dating and falling in love" house.
[h3]7th House Overlay[/h3]
The 7th house is the house of partnership and marriage. Significant planet overlays here often indicate long-term relationship potential.
[h3]8th House Overlay[/h3]
The 8th house governs intimacy, shared resources, and transformation. Planets here create deep, intense bonds — sometimes obsessive, always transformative. Mars in the 8th house synastry is one of the most intense placements for physical connection.
[h3]12th House Overlay[/h3]
The 12th house is spiritual and karmic. Connections here can feel fated, as if you knew each other in a past life. Sun in the 12th house synastry often indicates a hidden or deeply spiritual bond.
[h2]Red Flags in Synastry[/h2]
Not every connection is healthy. Watch for these challenging patterns:
[list]
[*][b]Pluto square personal planets[/b] — potential for manipulation and control
[*][b]Mars square Mars[/b] — frequent arguments and clashing wills
[*][b]Saturn opposite Venus[/b] — feeling unloved or emotionally restricted
[*][b]Neptune conjunct personal planets[/b] — idealization, deception, or confusion about the relationship
[/list]
These aspects do not doom a relationship, but they require awareness and maturity from both partners.
[h2]Beyond Sun Sign Compatibility[/h2]

[/list]
